About the Blog

Fangirl For The Week is a monthly music blog where I spend a week immersed in an artist's fandom space and detail my findings in a TikTok episode. It is a project launched with the aim of reigniting lost passion for fangirling, as well as creating a community of people who wish to reconnect with that side of themselves.
